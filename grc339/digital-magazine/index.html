Worn Wear: Fashion rehab with Simon Ungless

After years in high fashion with Alexander McQueen, Simon Ungless now creates one-of-a-kind sustainable designs on the California coast. Blending fine art with reclaimed materials, his work explores texture, memory, and environmental consciousness—challenging throwaway culture and revealing beauty in what’s been left behind.
