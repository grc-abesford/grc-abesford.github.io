Welcome to Bob’s Bagels, where passion meets dough! Our love for bagels is baked into every fibre of our being, and we’re delighted to share these delectable delights with you.

Nestled in the heart of Mill Valley, our restaurant is a haven for bagel enthusiasts. We’re on a mission to redefine the bagel experience, crafting each one with artisanal precision and a dash of creativity.

From classic New York-style to bold and innovative flavors, our menu boasts an array of freshly baked bagels, schmears, and curated toppings to tantalize your taste buds. Whether you crave a hearty breakfast, a satisfying lunch, or a quick snack on the go, our bagels are here to save the day.

At Bob’s bagels, we believe in quality, flavor, and community. We source the finest ingredients, handcraft each bagel with care, and welcome everyone to join us on this flavorful journey.
